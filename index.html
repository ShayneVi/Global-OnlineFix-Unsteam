Automated Steam Game Modification Tool

How to find your Steam App ID:

  1. Open Steam and go to your game's store page
  2. Look at the URL: store.steampowered.com/app/APPID/GameName
  3. Copy the number and paste it above

Example: Paint the Town Red = 337320

⚠️ Use this tool only for games you own. Always backup your game files first.
